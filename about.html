Who We Are

Toronto's Go-To Photo Booth Team

What started as a passion for capturing candid moments at events has grown into a full-service photo booth experience trusted by couples, corporate planners, and families across the Greater Toronto Area — from weddings and engagements to birthdays, graduations, and brand activations.

We're not just setting up equipment — we're creating an experience. From the moment we arrive to the last print of the night, our goal is to make your event a little more fun, a little more memorable, and a lot more shareable.

Every event we do, we treat like our own. That means meticulous setup, friendly on-site support, and photos your guests will actually want to keep.

What Makes Us Different

Professional Equipment

We use a professional Canon DSLR camera with studio-grade lighting — not a tablet or webcam. Your guests deserve real photo quality.

Two Dedicated Attendants

Every event gets two trained team members on-site to manage the booth, assist guests, and make sure everything runs seamlessly.

Fully Customized

Your event isn't cookie-cutter, and your photo booth shouldn't be either. We customize templates, backdrops, and props to match your theme.

Print + Digital

Guests get instant prints to take home AND digital copies to share. The best of both worlds — tangible memories and social-ready content.

Fast Communication

We know planning an event is stressful. That's why we respond quickly, answer every question thoroughly, and keep things simple.

GTA-Wide Coverage

Based in Toronto, serving Mississauga, Brampton, Vaughan, Markham, Oakville, Hamilton, and the entire GTA. From downtown venues to suburban banquet halls, we'll be there.
