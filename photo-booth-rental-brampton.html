Common Questions

Photo Booth Rental FAQ | Brampton

How much does a photo booth rental cost in Brampton?

Our packages start at $375 + HST for the Digital package (2 hours, digital sharing), which works well for smaller Brampton house parties and community gatherings. The Silver package starts at $625 with unlimited photo strip prints. Our most popular choice for Brampton weddings is the Gold package, starting at $750 with 3 hours and both photo strips and 4x6 cards — ideal for the large-scale receptions common at venues like Crystal Grand and Embassy Grand. The Diamond package starts at $875 with 4 hours and a custom photo album.

Do you provide photo booths for weddings in Brampton?

Absolutely! Weddings are our specialty, and Brampton is one of our busiest markets. We've provided photo booths at Crystal Grand Banquet Hall, Pearson Convention Centre, Embassy Grand Convention Centre, Le Jardin, and many more. Brampton weddings often have 300 to 800+ guests, and our setup handles high-volume events with ease. Our Gold and Diamond packages include custom print templates with your names, colours, and wedding date — perfect for multi-day celebrations like mehndi nights, receptions, and walimas.

What areas of Brampton do you serve?

We serve all of Brampton including Heart Lake, Bramalea, Mount Pleasant, Springdale, Castlemore, Gore Meadows, Bram West, Snelgrove, and surrounding areas. We also serve nearby cities like Mississauga, Vaughan, Caledon, and the entire GTA.

How far in advance should I book for a Brampton event?

We recommend booking 4-8 weeks in advance for most events. For Brampton weddings during peak season (May-October), we suggest 2-3 months in advance — weekends at popular venues like Crystal Grand, Embassy Grand, and Pearson Convention Centre book up quickly and so do we. However, we do accommodate last-minute bookings when available. Just reach out and we'll do our best!

What's included in every photo booth rental?

Every Instapix Events booking includes a professional Canon DSLR camera, studio lighting, custom-designed print templates, a curated props collection, an online gallery for easy sharing, and 2 dedicated attendants. Depending on your package, you'll also get unlimited prints, choice of backdrop, and our signature Glam Booth filter.

Do you offer corporate photo booth rentals in Brampton?

Yes! Brampton has a growing corporate scene and we work with companies of all sizes — from logistics firms and tech startups to retail grand openings and trade shows at the CAA Centre. We can add your company logo to the print templates and provide digital sharing links for social media. Our booth is a great fit for employee appreciation nights, holiday parties, and client-facing events in the Bramalea business corridor and beyond. Contact us for corporate pricing and multi-event discounts.
