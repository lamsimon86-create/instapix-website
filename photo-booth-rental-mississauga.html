Common Questions

Photo Booth Rental FAQ — Mississauga

How much does a photo booth rental cost in Mississauga?

Our packages start at $375 + HST for the Digital package (2 hours, digital sharing). The Silver package starts at $625 with unlimited photo strip prints. Our most popular wedding package, Gold, starts at $750 with 3 hours and both photo strips and 4x6 cards. The Diamond package starts at $875 with 4 hours and a custom photo album.

Do you provide photo booths for weddings in Mississauga?

Absolutely! Weddings are our specialty. We've provided photo booths at Mississauga Convention Centre, Terrace Banquet Centre, La Dome Banquet Halls, Crystal Fountain, and many more. Our Gold and Diamond packages include custom print templates with your names, colours, and wedding date.

What areas of Mississauga do you serve?

We serve all of Mississauga including Port Credit, Streetsville, Meadowvale, Erin Mills, Cooksville, Clarkson, Lorne Park, Malton, and surrounding areas. We also serve nearby cities like Brampton, Oakville, Burlington, and the entire GTA.

How far in advance should I book for a Mississauga event?

We recommend booking 4-8 weeks in advance for most events, and 2-3 months in advance for weddings during peak season (May-October). However, we do accommodate last-minute bookings when available — just reach out and we'll do our best!

What's included in every photo booth rental?

Every Instapix Events booking includes a professional Canon DSLR camera, studio lighting, custom-designed print templates, a curated props collection, an online gallery for easy sharing, and 2 dedicated attendants. Depending on your package, you'll also get unlimited prints, choice of backdrop, and our signature Glam Booth filter.

Do you offer corporate photo booth rentals in Mississauga?

Yes! We work with companies of all sizes for holiday parties, product launches, team events, and client appreciation events. We can add your company logo to the print templates and provide digital sharing links for social media. Contact us for corporate pricing and multi-event discounts.
